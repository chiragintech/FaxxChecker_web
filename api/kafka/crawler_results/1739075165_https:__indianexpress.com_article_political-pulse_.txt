Title: Arvind Kejriwal among top AAP losers as Congress gets more votes than BJP’s winning margin in 13 seats | Political Pulse News - The Indian Express
Description: Also affected were AAP’s Manish Sisodia, Saurabh Bharadwaj, Somnath Bharti and Durgesh Pathak
Content: Several Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) stalwarts, starting with Arvind Kejriwal, lost the Delhi Assembly elections by margins that were smaller than the votes won by the Congress in their seats. If Kejriwal lost from New Delhi, Manish Sisodia was defeated from Jangpura, Saurabh Bharadwaj from Greater Kailash, Somnath Bharti from Malviya Nagar, and Durgesh Pathak from Rajinder Nagar, all seats where the Congress got more votes than the winning margin of the BJP, thus affecting the result.
